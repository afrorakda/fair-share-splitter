# #94 Fair Splitter

A minimalist bill-splitting tool designed to eliminate the awkwardness of uneven cents and small change.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Focuses entirely on providing clear, actionable splitting scenarios.
- **Scenario-Based**: Doesn't just give one number; offers three logical ways to split the bill to help the group reach an agreement instantly.
- **Human-Centric**: Prioritizes legibility with large fonts and high-contrast UI, making it easy to read even in low-light restaurant settings.

## Key Features
- **Three-Way Logic**:
  - **Exact**: Pure mathematical division (no rounding).
  - **Round Down**: Rounds per person to the nearest whole number. Clearly states the "gap" amount for the representative to pay.
  - **Round Up**: Rounds per person up to the nearest whole number. Perfect for calculating tips or leaving extra change for the server.
- **One-Tap Copy**: Each scenario includes a "COPY" button that generates a polite, ready-to-paste message for chat apps (e.g., "Each: 25 (I'll cover the 2 gap!)").
- **Privacy First**: Browser-only operation. No login, no tracking, and no data leaves your device.

## Design System
- **Layout**: Single-column vertical stack for maximum readability and a clear information hierarchy.
- **Typography**: Oversized values (2rem) and bold descriptions (1rem) for instant recognition.
- **Interaction**: Clean monochrome design with a responsive "Copied!" notification anchored at the bottom for ergonomic accessibility.

## How to Use
1. **Input**: Enter the "Total Amount" and the "Number of People".
2. **Select**: Review the three scenarios (Exact, Round Down, Round Up).
3. **Action**: Tap "COPY" on the scenario that fits the group's mood.
4. **Share**: Paste the pre-formatted text into your group chat.
